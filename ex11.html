Wild Animals

  • Elephants are the world's largest land animal....
  • African Elephants and Asian Elephants are the two main species.
  • Asian Elephants are endangered.
  • Elephants communicate with vibrations through their feet.
  • Elephant family units are matriarchal.
  • Bears are carnivoran mammals of the family Ursidae.
  • They are classified as caniforms, or doglike carnivorans.
  • Although only eight species of bears are extant, they are widespread,
  • appearing in a wide variety of habitats throughout most of the Northern Hemisphere.
  • partially in the Southern Hemisphere.
  • Deer are specialized herbivores,
  • as is reflected in their large and anatomically complex digestive organs,
  • their mobile lips, and the size and complexity of their teeth.
  • However deer rely little on coarse-fibred grasses.
  • they have not evolved grazing specializations comparable to those found in bovids.
